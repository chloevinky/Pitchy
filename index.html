💗 Take care of your voice

Never push or strain to force a higher pitch — a feminine voice comes far more from resonance and ease than from height alone. If you feel strain, tension, pain, or hoarseness, stop and rest. For personalised, safe guidance consider working with a gender-affirming speech-language pathologist (SLP) or voice coach. This tool is an educational aid, not medical advice.

💡 Voice Training Tips

  • Prioritise resonance over raw pitch — bright, forward resonance reads more feminine than a high pitch alone
  • Practice gently and daily; never strain to reach a target
  • Focus on resonance placement - chest vs head voice
  • Work with a gender-affirming voice coach or SLP for personalized guidance
  • Record yourself regularly to track progress
  • Remember: pitch is just one aspect of gender expression
